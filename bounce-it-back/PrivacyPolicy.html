Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Bounce Back ("we," "us," or "our") handles information. We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing a free gaming experience.

2. Data Collected Locally

The Game stores certain data locally on your device, including:

  • High scores and personal bests.
  • In-game settings and preferences.
  • Game progression data. This data stays on your device and is not uploaded to our servers. Deleting the app will result in the permanent loss of this data.

3. Third-Party Advertising and Analytics

To fund the Game, we use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect and use specific data to provide personalized ads, including:

  • Device Identifiers: Such as the Apple IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers) or Android Advertising ID.
  • General Location Data: Based on IP addresses (not precise GPS).
  • Engagement Data: Which ads you clicked or viewed. For more information, please visit Google’s Privacy & Terms.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Since we do not collect personal information on our own servers, we do not store your data. You may reset your advertising identifiers through your device settings (iOS "Limit Ad Tracking" or Android "Opt out of Interest-Based Ads").

5. Children’s Privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated version within the Game.
